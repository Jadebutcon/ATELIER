A quick note on delivery

This store is lovingly managed by one person — from packing to shipping. Please allow a little extra time for your order to arrive. Every item is handled with care, and I truly appreciate your patience and support! 💛

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. Here's our policy.

Eligibility

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days from the date of delivery.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or purchased during clearance events are not eligible for returns or exchanges.
  • Undergarments, swimwear, and earrings are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

How to Return

1

Contact Us

Reach out via email or our contact form with your order code and reason for return.

2

Pack the Item

Securely pack the item in its original packaging with all tags still attached.

3

Ship It Back

Drop off the package at the designated courier. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer unless the item was defective or incorrect.

4

Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Exchanges

  • We offer size exchanges only for the same item and color, subject to availability.
  • If the desired size is unavailable, a full refund will be issued instead.
  • Exchange requests must be made within 7 days of delivery.
  • Color or style exchanges are treated as a return + new purchase.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue. We will arrange a free return and provide a full refund or replacement.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items worn, washed, or altered in any way
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Undergarments, swimwear, and earrings
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items returned after the 7-day window

Need Help with a Return?

Contact our support team and we'll guide you through the process.
